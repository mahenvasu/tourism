Kankesanthurai Beach (KKS Beach)


Kankesanthurai Beach, also known as KKS Beach, is a beautiful coastal area in Sri Lanka’s Jaffna District. belongs to the Northern Province, close to the small town of Kankesanthurai. KKS Beach is considered one of the best beaches in the northern part of the island.

Most of the beaches are located in the southern and eastern provinces of Sri Lanka. But you won’t find such beaches in the northern areas. Kankesanthurai Beach is a must-visit place if you are seeking the best places to travel in the Jaffna area.

Location and Climate of the Kankesanthurai Beach


Location


The distance to KKS Beach from Colombo is 373 km. It takes 8 hours and 30 minutes to reach there by bus or train. But it can reduce the time if you can reach there by car or van. However, the distance from Jaffna is nearly 20 kilometers. This normally takes 30 minutes to reach. Another enjoyable experience is traveling to Kankesanthurai by train. You can book the “Yal Devi” train from the Colombo Fort.

Climate of Kankesanthurai:

Kankesanthurai and the surrounding area are hotter than other places in the country. The annual average temperature is about 29C. The place receives an annual rainfall of between 1250 mm and 1500 mm. The rainy season extends from September to December. The majority of the year is dry. It is an ideal place for those who love hot and humid weather.



The beauty of KKS Beach


The place is managed by the Sri Lanka Navy. They have a camp near the beach. It is a marvelous beach with clear water. It is great for family get-togethers. You can walk a long distance towards the sea without sinking. Therefore, this is a great place to bathe and swim for anyone.

There is a big lighthouse near the beach. It generates great views that are awesome to capture with a camera. You can see colorful pictures in the mornings and evenings along with sunsets and sunrises. The beach is surrounded by palm trees, which cover the majority of the area. Swimming, jumping, and catching fish are some of the major activities that you can do at Kankesathurai Beach. As well, you can arrange a boat trip to the deep sea.

Normally, it is a less crowded place. You have enough space to enjoy it and relax. There are some sellers around the beach. You can buy essential things from those vendors.
